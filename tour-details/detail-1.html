Kerala 14 days package

Kerala

Day 1 Arrival in Kochi

  • Arrive at Kochi International Airport.
  • Check in to your 5 star hotel. .
  • Relax after your journey. .
  • Evening stroll at Fort Kochi; visit Chinese Fishing Nets.
  • Dinner at a local restaurant.














Day 2 Sightseeing in Kochi

  • Breakfast at the hotel.
  • Visit Mattancherry Palace and the Paradesi Synagogue.
  • Explore the Kerala Folklore Museum.
  • visit the famous jew street and do a bit of purchase.
  • Enjoy a Kathakali performance in the evening.












Day 3 Travel to Athirappally

  • Breakfast from hotel and check out.
  • Drive to Athirappally (approximately 1.5 hours)
  • Explore the Kerala Folklore Museum.
  • Explore nearby Vazhachal Waterfalls.
  • Check in to your waterfall resort and relax.













Day 4 Explore Athirappally

  • Breakfast at the resort.
  • Trekking around the waterfalls and nearby forest areas.
  • Visit the local village and experience rural life.
  • Afternoon relaxation at your hotel.
  • Evening visit to the waterfalls for sunset.













Day 5 Travel to Munnar

  • Breakfast at the resort.
  • Drive to Munnar (approximately 4 hours).
  • Check in to your hill view resort.
  • Visit the Tea Museum.
  • Evening walk in the local market or visit a tea estate.













Day 7 More of Munnar

  • Breakfast at the resort.
  • Visit Eravikulam National Park to see the Nilgiri Tahr.
  • Explore Mattupetty Dam; enjoy boating.
  • Visit the Rose Garden and take a leisurely walk through tea plantations
  • Evening at leisure.













Day 6 Exploring Munnar

  • Breakfast from resort and check out.
  • Drive to Thekkady (approximately 3.5 hours).
  • Check in to your lake resort
  • Check in to your lake resort
  • Check in to your lake resort












Day 8 Travel to Thekkady

  • Breakfast at the resort
  • Day trip to Top Station for stunning views.
  • Visit Attukal Waterfalls and enjoy nature.
  • Optional: Attend a Kalaripayattu martial arts show in the evening.
  • Evening at leisure.












Day 9 Thekkady Activities

  • Early breakfast from resort and check out.
  • Drive to Alleppey (approximately 4 hours).
  • Check in to the beach resort.
  • Explore the beach; enjoy a traditional Kerala lunch.
  • Visit local villages and relax in the beach resort with a beautiful sunset.














Day 10 Travel to Alleppey

  • breakfast and check out from resort directly to houseboat .
  • Full day of cruising through the serene backwaters.
  • Visit paddy fields and coconut groves.
  • Lunch served on board; relax and enjoy the scenery.
