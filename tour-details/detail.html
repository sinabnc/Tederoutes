Kerala 7 days package

Kerala

Day 1 Arrival in Kochi

  • Arrive at Kochi International Airport.
  • Check in to your 5 star hotel. .
  • Relax after your journey. .
  • ⁠Evening stroll at Fort Kochi; visit Chinese Fishing Nets.
  • Dinner at a local restaurant.













Day 2 Sightseeing in Kochi

  • ⁠Breakfast at the hotel.
  • Visit Mattancherry Palace and the Paradesi Synagogue.
  • Explore the Kerala Folklore Museum.
  • visit the famous jew street and do a bit of purchase.
  • Enjoy a Kathakali performance in the evening.













Day 3 Travel to Munnar

  • Breakfast from hotel and check out.
  • ⁠Drive to Athirappally (approximately 1.5 hours)
  • Explore the Kerala Folklore Museum.
  • ⁠Explore nearby Vazhachal Waterfalls.
  • ⁠Check in to your waterfall resort and relax.













Day 4 Exploring Munnar

  • ⁠Breakfast at the resort.
  • Drive to Munnar (approximately 4 hours).
  • Check in to your hill view resort.
  • Visit the Tea Museum.
  • Evening walk in the local market or visit a tea estate.













Day 5 Travel to Alleppey

  • Breakfast at the resort.
  • ⁠Visit Eravikulam National Park to see the Nilgiri Tahr.
  • Explore Mattupetty Dam; enjoy boating.
  • Visit the Rose Garden and take a leisurely walk through tea plantations
  • Evening at leisure.













Day 6 Alleppey Houseboat Experience

  • Full day of cruising through the backwaters.
  • Visit paddy fields and coconut groves.
  • Lunch served on board.
  • ⁠ Optional activities: fishing or canoeing.
  • Enjoy a traditional Kerala dinner on the houseboat.













Day 7 Return

  • ⁠Breakfast on the houseboat.
  • ⁠Check out and head back to Kochi (approximately 1.5 hours)
  • Last-minute shopping or sightseeing if time permits
  • Depart from Kochi International Airport.
